Hi, Im Said Oudouane

... and I'm a geek

Tech is reshaping how we learn and come together. Software is ubiquitous and every person can now build their way to a future that inspires them. However, today’s world isn’t designed to work for these makers creating an imbalance between the impact they’re capable of, and what’s expected of them.

We at YouCode learn how to solve this problem...

  • By learning best practices at YouCode through extensive mentoring;
  • By building communities, and networking with each other through our environment and even through events with other schools including 1337 and other schools;

To join us at YouCode you have to go through multiple stages and tests with other students.

These are the following stages:

  1. Online Test
  2. Face-To-Face test
  3. SaS period (3 weeks mentoring with a final project where you show what you learned during this period)

after that you get to continue for 2 years mentoring to develop your skills and become a developer.
